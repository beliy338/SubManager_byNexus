# SubManager - User Guide

## Welcome to SubManager! 🎉

SubManager is a subscription management application that helps you track all your subscriptions in one place with powerful analytics and cloud synchronization.

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account

1. Click **"Sign Up"** on the login page
2. Enter your name, email, and password (minimum 6 characters)
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the dashboard

### 2. Your First Login

When you first log in, you'll see a welcome message with helpful tips. All your data is now stored securely in the cloud and will be available on any device where you log in.

## Features

### Dashboard 📊

Your main hub showing:
- **Monthly Total**: Total cost of all subscriptions per month
- **Yearly Forecast**: Projected annual spending
- **Upcoming Billing**: Number of subscriptions billing in the next 7 days
- **Recent Subscriptions**: Quick view of your active subscriptions

### Add Subscription ➕

Click the **"+ Add Subscription"** button to add a new subscription:
- Name (e.g., "Netflix", "Spotify")
- Category (Streaming, Software, Gaming, etc.)
- Price
- Billing Cycle (Monthly or Yearly)
- Next Billing Date
- Status (Active, Cancelled, Paused)

## Cloud Synchronization ☁️

### What's Stored in the Cloud?

All your data is automatically saved to the cloud:
- ✅ All subscriptions
- ✅ Personal settings
- ✅ User preferences
- ✅ Account information

### Benefits

1. **Access Anywhere**: Log in from any device and see your data
2. **Automatic Backup**: Never lose your subscription data
3. **Real-time Sync**: Changes are instantly saved
4. **Secure**: Each user's data is completely isolated
5. **No Manual Saving**: Everything saves automatically

### How It Works

1. You make a change (add subscription, change settings, etc.)
2. The app saves it to the cloud instantly
3. When you log in from another device, your data is there
4. No need to export/import - it just works!

## Tips & Tricks

### Best Practices

1. **Keep It Updated**: Add subscriptions as soon as you subscribe
2. **Set Accurate Dates**: Ensure billing dates are correct for accurate forecasts
3. **Review Regularly**: Check the Analytics page monthly to understand your spending
4. **Use Categories**: Properly categorize subscriptions for better insights
5. **Check Upcoming Bills**: Review the "Upcoming Billing" section weekly

### Managing Multiple Currencies

If you have subscriptions in different currencies:
1. Add subscriptions with their original currency
2. The app automatically converts them to your preferred currency
3. Change your display currency anytime in Settings

### Privacy & Security

- Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text
- Each user's data is completely isolated from others
- All communications use secure HTTPS
- Your email is only used for authentication

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?

- Check your email and password are correct
- Password must be at least 6 characters
- Try refreshing the page

### Data Not Showing?

- Make sure you're logged in
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page

### Settings Not Saving?

- Ensure you're connected to the internet
- Look for the success notification
- Try changing the setting again

### Forgot Password?

Currently, password reset is not available. Please create a new account or contact support.
